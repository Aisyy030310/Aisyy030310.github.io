Fluxr Platform Goes Live

Posted 12 June 2026 by Siti Aminah

After months of development, the Fluxr website is ready for the public. Users can sign up, create profiles, browse the gallery and post on the home feed.

Our development team focused on speed and ease of use. The site works on desktop and mobile, with pages for messaging, pricing and account settings.

We will roll out new features every month. Send your feedback to hello@fluxr.my or use the contact form on this site.
