Privacy Policy

How Fluxr handles your data

Fluxr Sdn Bhd respects your privacy. This page explains what we collect, how we use it and what you can control.

Last updated: June 2026

When you register on Fluxr, we collect your name, email address and profile details such as photo and bio. When you use the platform, we also store posts, messages and photos you upload to your feed and gallery.

Your data is used to run Fluxr features including login, messaging, friend lists and the photo gallery. We use account information to personalise your profile and display your activity on the home feed. Fluxr does not sell personal information to third parties.

We only share data when required by law or when you choose to make content public on your profile. Service providers who help us host the platform may process data on our behalf under strict confidentiality agreements.

You can edit your profile details at any time from your account settings. To request account deletion or a copy of your data, contact us at privacy@fluxr.my. Passwords are stored using standard hashing methods and are never stored in plain text.

Fluxr uses cookies to keep you logged in and remember your preferences such as dark mode and language. We take reasonable steps to protect your data from unauthorised access, loss or misuse.

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a new date. For questions about privacy, email privacy@fluxr.my or visit our contact page.
